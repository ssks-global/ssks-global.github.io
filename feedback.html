Guidlines
Pre requisites

You should have google email account to participate in the discussion.

Login into google account in another tab in the same browser in which you are accessing this website.

Join discussion

Once you login into your google account in other Tab and comeback to this page, You will notice a button to click like Apply to join group besides the group name forum.

You will get a pop-up with optional inputs recommented to select for easy identification for authorities to accept the join request and click the Join the group button.

Your request will be verified and approved/rejected by the administrator which will be notified to you via email. You can also check the status by revisiting this forum page after few hours.

If you are accepted as member, you will see verious options to create a Topic, reply to the post etc.

Post recommendations

Select the topic from the topic's list.

Search if the topic already has posts relavent to the content you wish to post. It is recommended to reploy for the existing post if the content is already in discussion.

Create a new post with details you wish to convey and discuss with other group members.

Post via Email

You can post your content to the forum by emailing to ssks-global-forum@googlegroups.com.

It is recommended to provide only the topic name in the subject for which you are posting the content. This creates a post directly in to the relavent topic and avoid's creating dupplicate topics.
